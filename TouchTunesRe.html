TouchTunes

Tap fingers together to change the song/volume.

info

A wearable that sends a signal via bluetooth from a Teensy 3.1 to an android app to change a song. Clicking different fingers signals a different command on the app such as next song, previous song, stop, and pause. The idea is to be able to control your music while you run without having to take your phone out.

specific features
  • Change songs by clicking together certain fingers
  • Change volume by clicking together certain fingers
