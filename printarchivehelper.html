UCLA Print Archive

The goal of the site is to have one place where a reader can easily access the day's paper from any day in the Daily Bruin's history. The tricky thing is most of the Daily Bruin's history is stored in pdf reels up to 600 pages long . Some of the archive.org reels cover multiple years, while others cover only a few months.Searching for a date in the middle of the reel, which contains different newspapers of different page lengths, is a challenge.

Currently, my team is working with the Google Vision API to try and extract the dates for each page. So far, we have successfully been able to extract the dates on a small sample reel! One problem we have run into is the formatting of the date on each page. For cover pages, we can count on the full date being on the page; however, for the remaining pages of the paper, sometimes the year is not included in the date.. some pages don't have dates at all. We are working on a script that will "guess" the date of the page based on the previous cover pages date.
